The Hustle and Bustle Update: Part I

Yes, we have decided to split the Hustle and Bustle Update in two to release all the new content and everything we've done so far as Micro's first minor-level update!

Bringing you:

A Menu, with save selection and settings!

A Progressive Web App, installable and offline-compatible!

A Dark Theme, bringing new light (or the lack thereof) to Micro!

A Revamped UI, giving important things their priority!

A Bigger World, currently lacking content but bringing new adventures!

A Ton of Bug Fixes, polishing the Micro experience!

A Soundtrack to Come, alongside expansion content in the Hustle and Bustle Update Part II!

0.0.3

The Combat Update

This world isn't safe for us anymore...

Enemies roam the outside lands.

In fear, many wanderers pass by this place...

But it need not always be so.

Encounter Enemies

Find enemies outside the workshop area.

Fight them to receive resources... or lose them all trying.

Protect the Wanderers

After your first fight, you'll be able to collect resources to build a wall to protect the workshop area and encourage wanderers to stop by more often.

With more workers, your resource production will improve...

Gear Up

Find a building in the wild that turns your now-plentiful materials into greater resilience and strength in battle.

Fight stronger enemies the farther out you get...

Greater Rewards

Another building in the wilderness will turn enemy drops and extra wanderers into more powerful enemies.

Will you fight them to obtain greater rewards?

Or will your grinding go to waste...

The Other Side

A special item from the aforementioned building can be very helpful...

Discover its uses and travel across the void...
