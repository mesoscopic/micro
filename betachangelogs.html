0.0.3.4

  • Further revised the new layout, so that the info box is as large as it needs to be and the world shrinks accordingly
  • Reworked the versioning system, including adding a dialog box when new updates come out (that might have led you here!)
  • This hasn't been implemented, but there are potential plans for contact-based combat:
    • Attacks would stop when and if they hit the other belligerent, doing damage and animating the attacker back
    • This would make combat more interesting, especially for enemies of different attack speeds
    • Attack speed may be changeable in the armory, maybe as a tradeoff for damage?
    • Bosses (and maybe some normal enemies depending on how powerful it is) may gain a dodging ability
    • Basically, if one attacks the other, it does damage exactly like normal
    • But if they meet in the middle, they both take damage and return to their starting positions
    • Obviously, dodging prevents the enemy from hitting you
    • We're open to feedback on this potentially upcoming combat mechanic (I know this should have been in 0.0.3 but I didn't think of it until now)
